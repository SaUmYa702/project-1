Key Insights

Sector Dominance

Crop-related queries dominate the dataset, followed by livestock and irrigation sectors, indicating the primary focus areas of farmer concerns.

Category Patterns

Technical queries are most common in crop sector, while policy-related queries dominate in irrigation and livestock sectors.

Seasonal Impact

Query patterns show strong seasonal variations, with crop-related queries peaking during planting and harvesting seasons.
