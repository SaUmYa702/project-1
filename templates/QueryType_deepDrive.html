Key Insights

Technical Focus

Technical queries dominate the dataset, with pest control and crop management being the most common concerns among farmers.

Resolution Patterns

Technical queries show faster resolution times compared to policy-related queries, indicating a need for streamlined policy information access.

Seasonal Variations

Query types show distinct seasonal patterns, with technical queries peaking during planting and harvesting seasons.
